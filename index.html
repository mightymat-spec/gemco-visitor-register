Your Privacy

Gemco Players Community Theatre

Before you check in, here's how we handle your information — simply and honestly.
📋
Why we collect itWe're required to keep a visitor register under our Victorian liquor licence. That's the only reason.
🚫
We won't spam youYour details are never used for marketing, shared with third parties, or sold. Ever.
📱
Stored on your deviceYour details are saved on your phone so you don't have to re-enter them next visit.
🔐
Secure & privateCheck-in records are stored securely and only accessible to authorised Gemco Players members.
▶ Why is this safe? Concerned about online forms?
🇦🇺
Data stays in AustraliaYour information is stored in Google Sheets on Google's Australian servers — the same infrastructure used by Australian banks, hospitals, and government agencies.
🔑
No passwords or financial infoWe only ask for your name, email, phone and suburb — nothing sensitive. We never ask for passwords, payment details or ID numbers.
👁️
Only authorised members can see itThe register is a private Google Sheet accessible only to authorised Gemco Players members — not the public.
📜
It's the lawVisitor registers are a legal requirement under the Liquor Control Reform Act 1998 (Vic). Paper registers carry the same information — this is simply a digital version.
By checking in you agree to the collection of your details for liquor licence compliance purposes only.
